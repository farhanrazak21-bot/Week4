Number Patterns — Interactive (0–20)

Five lessons: Counting, Count by 2s, Bundles & Skip Counting, Even/Odd, Weekly Review
Start each lesson with a short dua and a warm greeting.
Connect to Islamic reflections and gratitude (Alhamdulillah).

Teacher Notes

  • Use real objects (socks, pencils, cubes) for hands-on practice.
  • More able students: count beyond 20, find missing two numbers, count by 5s or 10s.
  • Less able students: use number line 0–10 and one-to-one matching with counters.
  • Plenary ideas: chants, pass-the-ball (real class), quick oral quiz.
